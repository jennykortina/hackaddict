In comes DivShare.

DivShare is a free file hosting service for everyone. No registration required!
  • Upload unlimited files
  • Serve unlimited downloads
  • Files stay online forever!
  • No pop-up ads or spam
  • Incredibly fast, reliable servers
I signed up for an account last night and so far it looks pretty awesome. They have a neat file organization system and it's super simple to use. So far it's a great file sharing solution.
