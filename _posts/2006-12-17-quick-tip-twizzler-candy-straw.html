--- layout: post title: 'DIY Straw: Twizzler = Candy Straw' date: '2006-12-17T21:02:00.000-05:00' author: jenny tags: - diy modified_time: '2006-12-30T12:20:24.003-05:00' thumbnail: http://bp0.blogger.com/_Gj3xvk4ycVs/RYX4loVviiI/AAAAAAAAAB0/qYHUgvZSMMk/s72-c/twizz.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5518298822864690168.post-759509161005866264 blogger_orig_url: https://www.hackaddict.net/2006/12/quick-tip-twizzler-candy-straw.html --- I went to the movies with a friend of mine today and we got some Twizzlers and waters. I randomly ask him if he's ever used a twizzler as a straw and he says no. So I make one. Here's a quick tutorial for the utilitarian treat:

1. Bite the near end off the Twizzler
2. Bite the far end off the Twizzler
3. Insert Twizzler into beverage and enjoy.
